Visit Schitt's Creek!

Roland Schitt, Mayor of Schitt's Creek

As the proud Mayor of (and part-Motel owner in) Schitt's Creek, I formally invite you to visit our little slice of paradise!

Schitt's Creek has a little bit of something for everyone. Do you like aesthetic-forward, locally-made hand creams? Shop at the Rose Apothecary! Do you like food? Dine at Twyla's Cafe Tropical! Do you have a car? Maybe Bob can fix it at Bob's Garage!

And the fun doesn't stop there. Schitt's Creek is home to local celebrities like the Jazzagals (including vocals from Sunrise Bay actress and former LarryAir representative Moira Rose) and Alexis Rose, the singer/songwriter/aspiring PR executive/jet-setter behind the one-hit-wonder "A Little Bit Alexis"! Rest assured that Schitt's Creek is filled with culture and entertainment.

Book your stay at the Rosebud Motel today and experience all that Schitt's Creek has to offer for yourself!
